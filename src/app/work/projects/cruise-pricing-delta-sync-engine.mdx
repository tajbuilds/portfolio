## Overview

An automated data comparison workflow that normalizes supplier feeds, matches items to internal records, and emits change sets for review and publishing.

## Highlights

- Normalization rules for messy supplier schemas.
- Daily delta detection for additions, removals, and price updates.
- Alerting and reporting for safe review before publish.

## Tech

JavaScript, Postgres, APIs, n8n.

## Outcome

Reduced manual checks and enabled reliable daily refreshes.
